last.fm Friend Rank

For shits and giggles, you can now access this app as a chat bot.

Instructions...

  1. Add lastfm-friend-rank@appspot.com as a buddy on Google Talk, Jabber, or any other XMPP client you can think of.
  2. Say /user username where username is the name of some user on last.fm.
  3. That's it!
